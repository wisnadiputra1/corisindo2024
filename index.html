FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the trip plans?

Our AI chatbot is designed to provide accurate, well-rounded trip plans based on your preferences. However, you’re always in control and can adjust or fine-tune your itinerary.

Does it work for group trips?

Yes, our chatbot can handle group trip planning for friends, families, or even office retreats. Just share the details, and it’ll do the heavy lifting.

Can it adapt to my budget?

Absolutely! Just specify your budget while chatting with the bot, and it’ll recommend attractions, accommodations, and more that fit within your financial constraints.

Can I change plans halfway?

Flexibility is key! Our AI chatbot can make on-the-fly adjustments to your trip schedule—even when you're on the move—so you can live life on your terms.
