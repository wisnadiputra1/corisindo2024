Hotel

Based on your budget and preferences, here are a few hotel recommendations for your upcoming trip to Bali:

Padma Resort Ubud is a luxurious resort set in the heart of Ubud. It features stunning views of the surrounding rice fields and mountains.

Kappa Senses Ubud is a more intimate resort with a focus on wellness. It offers a variety of spa treatments and yoga classes.

Radisson Blu Resort is a great option for families. It features a waterpark, a kids' club, and a variety of restaurants.

RIMA by AYANA is a luxurious resort located on the beach in Jimbaran. It features stunning views of the Indian Ocean and a variety of restaurants.

Destination

Here is a suggested destination schedule for your day 1 in Bali:

Morning: Start your day with a sunrise hike up Mount Batur. This is a great way to see the beautiful views of the volcano and the surrounding area.

Afternoon: In the afternoon, visit the Waterfalls and Temples Tour. This tour will take you to some of the most beautiful waterfalls in Bali, as well as some of the island's most important temples.

Evening: In the evening, enjoy a snorkeling and kayaking adventure. This is a great way to see the underwater world of Bali, and it's also a lot of fun.

Night: At night, cap off your day with a Kecak Fire & Dance Show. This is a traditional Balinese dance performance that is sure to be a memorable experience.
